Product Design

At eShocan, our innovative engineers develop manufacturable, cost-effective new products. Our product development team plans each stage of the product life cycle--conceptualization, design, and development of parts and components--to provide a high-quality deliverable with a quick turnaround.

Simulation

Product development can be costly and time-consuming. To avoid waste, we reduce the product life cycle cost and timeline with computer-aided engineering (CAE) tools. With CAE virtual simulations, we simplify design iterations and limit the number of necessary physical prototypes, making it easier for engineers to improve vehicle performance.
