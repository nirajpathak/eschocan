Virtual Simulation

Product development can be costly and time-consuming. To avoid waste, we reduce the product life cycle cost and timeline with computer-aided engineering (CAE) tools. With CAE virtual simulations, we simplify design iterations and limit the number of necessary physical prototypes, making it easier for engineers to improve vehicle performance.

How we can help

Predictive Simulation

Technology is our passion we'll help you accurately predict real-world performance early to reduce development risk. Our engineering group experienced in almost every simulation discipline you can imagine.

Optimizing Performance

We'll help you find the optimal balance between weight, performance, and cost for your products. From software proficiencies, concept architecture studies to design for additive manufacturing and multi-disciplinary optimization projects, eShocan can be your partner.

Saving Time

Our goal is to free up time for you to innovate and shorten time-to-market. We work with your engineering teams to identify inefficiencies in the simulation process and suggest tailored modifications that address these challenges without disrupting established processes
