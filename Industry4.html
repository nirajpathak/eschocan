Digital
Manufacturing
and Assembly

From product introduction to long-term production, digital manufacturing is a crucial part of product life cycle management (PLM).

Together with computer-aided design (CAD), computer-aided manufacturing (CAM), computer-aided engineering (CAE), and product data management (PDM), digital manufacturing can optimize entire product life cycles for suitability, reliability and profitability. This alignment allows manufacturing companies to achieve their time-to-market and volume goals, as well as realize cost savings by reducing expensive downstream changes. We offer manufacturers the complete range of manufacturing processes in a virtual environment, including tooling, assembly lines, work centers, facility layout, ergonomics, and resources.
